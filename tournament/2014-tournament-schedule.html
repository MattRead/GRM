2014 Tournament Schedule

There will be 10 teams this year, split into 2 Divisions.

The top 2 teams in each division (4 total teams) will advance to the semi-finals on Sunday (Tie Breaker Rules will be used to determine top 2 seeds).

The Winners of the Semi-Finals move on to the Finals.

Tie Breakers Rules

For the purpose of "seeding" teams, a tie in standings between two teams will be resolved by, in this order:

  1. Points
  2. Wins
  3. Head to Head
  4. Plus / Minus
  5. Runs Against
  6. Coin Flip
