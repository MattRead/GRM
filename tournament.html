Tournament

The tournament begins on Friday, July 28th at the Carp Fairgrounds in Carp, Ontario and continues throughout the weekend, ending on Sunday, July 30th, 2017. A day pass is $5 and a weekend pass is $10 with kids under 12 free.

The updated schedule is now available.

There will be 10 Teams this year:

Group A

  • Micksburg, ON Twins
  • West Carleton Electric - Fitzroy Harbour, ON
  • Party Lake - Sydenham, ON
  • Goodfellow's Fastpitch - Eastons Corners, ON
  • Ottawa Valley A's - Carp, ON

Group B

  • Kanata, ON Pirates
  • Pickering, ON Panthers
  • Elkland, PA - Moose Lodge #746
  • Broker4Tickets - Ottawa, ON
  • Napanee, ON Intermediate Express

Be sure to follow our Twitter Feed for up-to-date tournament results.
